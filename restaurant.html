About Us

Our menus are shaped by the seasons, crafted with care, and inspired by the incredible produce found right on our doorstep.

We work closely with local farmers, foragers, and fishermen to bring the freshest ingredients to your plate. From wild venison and line-caught seafood to just-picked garden herbs and vegetables.

Whatever you're here for, Sage & Salt's offering is honest, seasonal, and deeply satisfying.
