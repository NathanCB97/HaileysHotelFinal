ABOUT US

Our Story

Hailey's Hotel first opened its doors nearly 50 years ago. Founded by Hailey, whose vision was to create a welcoming retreat inspired by the warmth and character of traditional Scottish hospitality, the hotel began as a humble guesthouse and quickly became a local treasure.


Over the decades, it has grown into the boutique hotel it is today — loved for its unique blend of vintage charm and modern comfort. Still family-run, the legacy continues with Hailey Jr., who is now stepping into her mother's shoes, carrying forward the spirit, care, and heart that have defined Hailey's from the very beginning.

Hailey
